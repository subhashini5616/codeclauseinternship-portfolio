Recent Projects

Sentiment Analysis of Social Media Data

I performed sentiment analysis on a large dataset of social media posts to understand customer sentiments towards a leading consumer brand. Using natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning, I classified posts into positive, negative, or neutral sentiments. The insights gained from this analysis helped the brand make informed decisions about product improvements and marketing campaigns.

Market Basket Analysis for Retail

I conducted a market basket analysis for a retail chain to uncover purchasing patterns and cross-selling opportunities. By analyzing transaction data, I identified which products were frequently purchased together. This analysis enabled the retailer to optimize product placements and create targeted promotions, resulting in increased sales and improved customer satisfaction.

Financial Forecasting for Investment Firm

I conducted financial forecasting for an investment firm, using time series analysis and predictive modeling techniques. I analyzed historical financial data to predict future market trends and asset performance. My accurate forecasts helped the firm make well-informed investment decisions, leading to substantial financial gains for their clients.

Healthcare Data Analysis
